Azuraki's Notes

  • This is a guide for all 5* demons in the game.
  • Its focus is in analyzing and discussing what are good/bad archetypes and is based on looking at their kit + how the playerbase generally builds them.
  • There may be people who have better opinions than mine as I don't have that many 5* demons.

  • Brand setups:
    • Primary = Main stat found on a brand. Always aim for a % stat for Arms and Leg brands.
      • 1. Head = HP+
      • 2. Right Arm = Crit %, Accuracy %, Evasion %
      • 3. Left Arm = Ailment Infliction %, Ailment Resistance %
      • 4. Body = PDEF+, MDEF+
      • 5. Legs = Speed %, Healing Amount %
      • 2,3,5 also has = HP (+/%), PATK (+/%), PDEF (+/%), MATK (+/%), MDEF (+/%)
    • Secondary = Second stat.
      • Possibilities: +HP, +PATK, +PDEF, +MATK, +MDEF.
      • Ideally you'd want to match primary stat. E.g. PATK% primary with PATK+ secondary.
    • Tertiary = Rest of the stats. Aim for complementing stats with the build in mind.
    • Physical
      • Primary = PATK %
      • Tertiaries = PATK %, Critical %, Accuracy %
      • War / Ward OR Divine / Ward
    • Magical
      • Primary = MATK %
      • Tertiaries = MATK %, HP%, Evasion %
      • Spell / Ward OR Divine / Ward
    • Support
      • Primary = HP%, Evasion % (If Demon has good agility), Ailment Infliction % (If you're using an ailment demon)
      • Tertiaries = HP%, Evade%, MDEF%, PDEF%
      • Life / Ward OR Divine / Ward

[Metatron]

Highest grade demon with the unique skill Fire Of Sinai (FoS). FoS hits really hard with guaranteed 5 hits (+1 if you're using Eileen enabling you to activate her skills), and so quite amazing for boss killing. He comes naturally to boot with Infinite Chakra for extra mana so you would be able to spam tons of spells with him without needing divine brands. He's the hardest to fuse as the only combination to make him is Vishnu + Barong and for this reason i wouldn't fuse him away (regardless of his archetype, his base kit works wonders).

Common Enduring Soul increases his survivability, ensuring he lives means ensuring he can kill. You can easily stack this with Endure to make him really hard to kill.
This archetype is the hardest to obtain as you can't rely on gacha pulls to get components and must fuse from scratch (Unless you get lucky with Hell Buns). 		Best
Aragami Mahamadyne gives him an aoe to use (though is restricted to light). This is amazing for content such as Sloth 10 and Aura Gate. Usable
Protector Offensive Cry is a great offensive buff (granting Tarukaja and Rakunda), he can set up the damage buff leaving your other sweepers to then follow up, hitting as hard as possible.
Sadly this is only usable for manual play, due to him being Attack AI he'd never use it on auto 		Usable
Psychic Mana Gain/Bonus is sadly a bad skill in general, there isn't really any reason to save up to 12 mp. Bad
Elementalist Given he has resist elec in general, Repel Elec for one upgrades it, but it doesn't really do much. The only case where this would help is when facing Thor with Elec Pierce.
Pair Metatron with Alilat and you can block elec sweeping. Its a pretty rare case and so it doesn't really warrant switching to Teal just for that purpose. 		Bad

Recommended skills:

  • Endure, easily stacks with Enduring Soul further adding to his survival.
  • Megido/Megidola/Megidolaon. For aoe sweeping, you can then use FoS to clean up/for single targets. Go Megido if you do pvp and want a turn one aoe, Megidolaon for turn 2 start/pve in general.
  • Serial Killer/Butcher. Not only does it affect Aoe skills, it also affects Multi-Hit, so you can power up Fire of Sinai's damage considerably.

Brands: Spell/Divine, whichever you want.

Protector You wouldn't really expect him to survive last/be a tank and so Recarmdra doesn't have much use. People would generally aim for him first, so its unlikely he'd survive to the point where he can use this Bad
Psychic Nothing else really beats Butcher. It is the only way to increase his sweeping damage due to Morning Star/Megidolaon being almighty. Best
Elementalist Drain Light would only be better if you manage to have gotten a Butcher skill genome. It covers his weakness allowing him to be healed instead (You'd want to cover his weakness anyways and so Resist Light would work regardless).

  • Resist Light. If not Teal to cover his weakness
  • Endure. Pvp mainly for extra survival.
  • Serial Killer to amp up his almighty damage.

Brands: Use Divine to ensure he can two turn Morning Star.

Yoshitsune

He is immune to light and dark, resistant to phys attacks, has high strength, good agility and decent (buffed recently) luck. He comes with the unique skill Hassou Tobi (8x random hits (pierces as well)), which is very useful for bosses and brand farming. His other innate skills include Vorpal Blade and Mana Aid, making him amazing in PVE and PVP. Mana Aid allows him to recover full MP each wave so he can sweep and repeat with Vorpal Blade or Hassou Tobi.

Common
Aragami Gigantomachia
God's Hand
Protector Charge allows you to ramp up your damage even further; a 5mp charge coupled with Templar Dragon's passive would hit really hard.
Auto-Sukuka is a decent auto buff that increases your accuracy and evasion, allowing you to hit phys attacks more often and dodge more often.
Psychic Phys Amp makes him hit really hard, ripping the enemies to shreds.
Savage Glee would add onto his crit rate meaning it'll be quite likely in you getting another turn.
Elementalist Drain Phys allows you to not have to worry about repelled Phys damage, greatly adding to his survivability.
Resist Ice can be replaced as he doesn't have an ice weakness.

Recommended skills:

  • Phys Boost/Amp. Give him more power.
  • Bloody/Savage Glee. Give him more crit (if not purple)
  • Good/Great Aim. If you find he's missing a lot this can help with accuracy issues. Though you should aim for accuracy % tertiaries from brands.
  • Charge (Optional). To pull off stuff like the Templar Dragon strategy.

Brands: War/Ward or Divine/Ward. Divine would be better for charge setups.

Mother Harlot

The third gacha exclusive demon in the game with 2 unique skills. She has Mana Gain (meh), Death Lust (+25% dmg to charmed enemies, 30% chance to charm all at the start of your turn) and Babylon Goblet (Almighty damage to all, 100 dmg and drains 1mp from each target). She punishes teams without Ward brands and for pve content has a chance to charm off the bat every wave. Babylon Goblet is amazing at sucking the mp out of the enemy team, using her in a Jack Bros setup you can rob them of all their mana and when its their turn they'd be unable to counter attack.

Insanity will increase her chances of charming enemies overall

Naturally she has Null Phys so she can stop a lot of phys users. Makara Shift is nice in playing defensively when you start second in pvp.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force. She has a weakness that should be covered.
  • Mahamarin Karin. Though i think it'd be better to have another demon charmed and have her focused at spamming Babylon Goblet such as Alilat, I have a guildie that runs this that does really well in pvp. Given her high luck she'd likely inflict it easily.

Ward if you're worried of her being inflicted by ailments. Shield would be good otherwise.

People don't really run Memutudi as well so they'd waste press turns waiting for mute to go away, this would also shut down healers if you get rid of Ward via barrier break.

Mot

The longer he's left alive, the more dangerous the enemy team becomes. Given that it takes two turns for a 10% jump, it'd take 10 turns to reach 50%. This is unlikely to be reached in pvp but in pve content such as Kiwami this can prove quite useful depending on who you go up against.

Izanami

A really strong magical demon with good survivability as well. She utilizes the unique skill Yomotsu Okami which, as long as she's alive Elec and Dark attacks do 10% more damage for all party members. This means she's useful for setups with demons such as Purple Thor (Elec pierce) and Black Frost (Go To Hell! has dark pierce attached to it). Even without them, she herself comes with Bolt Storm and Merciless blow meaning if you face a elec weak demon they'd get fried to a crisp.

Best Archetype(s):

  • All of her Archetypes are pretty good i'd say, the only one that's iffy to me is Teal(Repel Ice). Since she already nulls ice, the only use this will get is pairing her up with Alilat to ignore ice pierce. Though the only situation that this will be useful is if you face a Purple Rama which would be quite rare anyways.
  • Clear (Life Surge). You can increase her tankiness with this, though this'll be the hardest to get unless you're really luck with hell buns or have tons of mag to fuse clear 5* demons.
  • Red (Hell Gaze). Goes well with her unique passive and is quite powerful too, 180 power will hit hard.
  • Yellow (Concentrate). Enables you to turn her into a sweeper if you like. Though if you want to cover her weakness you'd only be able to pick one additional skill.
  • Purple (Elec Amp). Boosts her elec damage even further.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Fire. Weakness should be covered
  • Elec Boost. Goes well with Elec Amp, though if you take resist as well you'd be conjuring Bolt Storms all day and night Dark skill. To take advantage of her passive
  • Dark boost. Pair with Red or a dark skill if you don't want to cover her weakness.

Brands: Magic type is fine since Bolt Storm doesn't cost much. Hell Gaze will cost 6mp, go with Divine if you want to use it off the bat in pvp.

Alice

Dark oriented unit with Mamudoon, Mudodyne and her unique Die for Me! (Kills a single enemy, one use only). Yup, she can kill anyone she points her finger at (provided they aren't immune to dark/boss as they are immune). She's really good for shutting down dangerous opponents and can hit hard provided no dark immunity as that's all her base kit relies on.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow gives her a unique skill (Necro Dogma + Epic Recovery), though given its cost (9mp -> 8) and effect (Almighty damage 100 to all enemies + Rakukaja), it isn't that good as its a weak Megidolaon. Epic Recovery is sadly meh (its a rare skill and she's the only source of it, in case you are interested in it). The main appeal with this Archetype is that is gives her more of a supportive role, also saving you from transferring Megido, so you can go with Resist Light + whatever you want. You'd want to run Divine in order to be able to 2 turn Necro Dogma.
  • Purple (Dark Amp + Wild Instinct). Makes all of her dark skills hit even harder, Wild Instinct is pretty crappy i'd recommend removing it.
  • Teal (Repel Light + Thunder Reign). Covers her light weakness and gives her an alternate source of attack. Thunder Reign is quite powerful so it'll capitalize on her high magic stat.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Light (If not Teal) she should have her weakness covered.
  • Dark Boost to take support her kit
  • Megido as she only uses Dark skills, Almighty can be used when you face dark immune/resistant opponents.

Brands: Divine/Ward helps you charge up for Die For Me! as quickly as possible, though Spell would work as Mamudoon and Mudodyne both cost 6 mp only.

Huang Di

Another powerful hero with no weaknesses, high strength, high agility, luck and this time sporting 2 unique skills + Hades Blast. Superior Power (Passive) which boosts his accuracy + granting him Phys pierce (!!!) and Yinlong Killer, a sweeping phys skill which adds 50% crit with 150 power. With that skillset he's quite devastating in any content and this is probably why (him + Hell Biker + Zaou Gongen), you see a lot of anti-phys defense teams in pvp.

Best Archetype(s):

  • All are good due to him having amazing gacha skills. If you wanted to archetype change with him though, then either go with Purple for Null Charm (Pvp as charm is widely used) or Teal for Repel Light (Covers for Hecatoncheires' Light weakness when facing light sweeping demons like Mastema). So i'll just go over each archetype and why it works
  • Red (Berserker God + Auto-Taruka). Auto taruka is what makes this really nice, saves you using another demon with it though if you have Ananta this archetype isn't that good. Berserker God is good at taking out single targets but his main use is in sweeping anyhows
  • Yellow (Salvation + Null Phys). Salvation is not that good on him as he is not a caster, Null Phys is what makes this pulled archetype good (and is the only source of a Null resistance that is transferable). If his attack is repelled he won't die to the reflected damage so you can sweep teams using Alilat + repel phys.
  • Purple (Null Charm + Serial Killer). Immunity to charm is amazing in pvp as mentioned before (so you won't sweep your own kill). Having Serial Killer will boost his aoe damage even further.
  • Teal (Repel Light + Infernal Mask). Immunity to light also means you can't be one shotted by God's Bow, whilst Infernal Mask ensures he's unlikely to be hit by ailments in the first place.

Recommended skills:

  • Phys Boost. Damage up, sweeping all day and all night
  • Endure/Enduring Soul for more survival so he can bring the pain.

Brands: War to maximise his damage or Divine if you want to pull off turn 1 Hades Blast and have 2 turn Yinlong Killer spamming. People sometimes go with Life too to ensure he's bulky. You wouldn't really need Ward as Purple or pulled Teal though it can act as a safety harness. In that case Aim would be nice against evasion teams or Shield for magic defense.

Huang Long

One of the best support demons in the game (I want him >-<). His main use is for Five Elements which is a improved version of Makarakarn, reflecting all spells including Almighty ones. Only pierce skills/phys attacks can be used. Regardless of the archetype, i would recommend getting him as 5 Elements will make content much easier.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow (Luster Candy). In addition to casting 5E, he can buff your party to boost all stats, what's not to love about that?
  • Purple (Speedster). If you really need more speed, then he's your guy, with his naturally high speed your team would reach really high battle speed numbers, ensuring you act first.
  • Teal (Null Dark). Due to his amazing natural resistances, Null Dark would mean he'd resist all elements be immune to Light and Dark.

Recommended skills:

  • Endure. For pvp and survival
  • Samarecarm. Gives you more support utility
  • Barrier Break. Combine this with a Speedster HL for turn one ailment teams. On his subsequent turns you can then spam 5E for survival.
  • Speedster. If not purple and you'd like the skill anyways its still a valid choice on him. A fair investment though as it is 10 skill points and requires you to fuse Kama (or if you're lucky have a Teal pulled Rakshasha).
  • Resist Phys/Null Phys. If you're going for the Teal Route (which is what i want to do), this further bolsters him.
  • Dodge/Evade. You can also go for a evasion type build with him if you want. 5 Elements to ignore magic and evasion to avoid physical.

Brands: Divine type, you'd want to always have 5E up.

Rama

Another OP pvp demon with no weaknesses (Immune to fire, resistant to ice). Rama comes with 2 unique skills + Mabufudyne: Drain Pierce (Pierce has no effect on demons with Drain resistances, provided he's alive) and Brahmastra (Almighty hit all 3 times). He's the drain version of Alilat's effect and with Brahmastra is effective against demons that have endure/enduring soul.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Red is usable if you pulled him (Energy Drain + Mana Aid), though i wouldn't recommend archetype switching to him as Energy Drain is lackluster. Mana Aid makes him really good in pve, essentially you'd wipe out waves with Brahmastra, regain your mana, rinse and repeat. This would make him a really good demon to use in autoing provided he hits hard enough.
  • Purple (Ice Pierce + Ice Age). The only source of ice pierce, being gacha exclusive. Though you'd want to spam Brahmastra, ice pierce would laugh in the face of 5 elements and you wouldn't have to bring Makara Break in those cases.
  • Yellow (Luster Candy + Concentrate). Puts him more into a balanced role with better support and damage. Concentrate would allow you to use him in Jack Bros setups whilst Luster Candy would give you another source of buff all (Though that's better suited to Amaterasu/Huang Long).

Recommended skills:

  • Endure/Enduring soul. As long as he's alive you want Brahmastra spam.
  • Butcher/Serial Killer. The only way to boost his almighty damage, though butcher is only obtainable through Skill gacha. Serial killer can be gotten from Shiva.
  • Ice Boost. If you're insistent on using him as an ice piercer, this can be boost up Mabufudyne's damage.

Brands: Divine/Ward. You want to spam spells so this is the only option really (Spell is okay but then you wouldn't be able to 2 turn skills).

Zaou-Gongen

One of the key pieces in the Rebellion + Pierce combo (Pair him with Hell Biker). This is due to his unique skill Kongo Zaou which enables demons with Rebellion to also have pierce attached to this phys skills.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Red (Herculean Strike). Only when you have Hell Biker with you, gives you a sweeping skill to use which is spammable too without needing divine brands.
  • Yellow (Rebellion). Without Hell Biker you can still take advantage of his passive, so he himself can still be a really effective phys demon.
  • Teal (Drain Phys). Only when you have Rama, though i'm not sure how viable Rama would be in a Biker Team (as i don't have a Hell Biker). I think Biker v Biker pvp matches would likely swing in your favour.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Elec to cover his weakness
  • Phys Aoe skill if not red.

Brands: Phys type or Divine Type. Divine allows you to use God's Hand more and you would be able to use more expensive aoe skills.

Alilat

Really good defensive demon to use in pvp, helping immensely vs piercing units (such as those that can be seen in AG). This is because of her unique skill Repel Pierce which makes ignores the pierce effect on units that repel the attribute. She also has really high luck + Infernal Mask meaning she can inflict ailments quite well/be really unlikely to be inflicted by ailments herself. Due to this you have a lot of freedom in picking how you want to build her, whether a debuffer, ailment caster, or even a healer too.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Common. The only archetype available so really no choice.

Recommended skills:

  • Samarecarm. For added support.
  • Recarmdra. To surprise people in pvp and give your team a second chance.
  • Aoe ailment skills except for curse and weak. Charm all, Bind all, Mute all are all valid (This is mainly for pvp)
  • War Cry/Fog Breath/Acid Breath, she can act as a tanky debuffer.

Brands: Life/Shield. Aim for Life%, or ailment % if you're using Ailment skills. Some people go with Divine to ensure turn 1 ailment casts. PDEF % is also valid when you're fighting against Hell Biker teams.

Lakshmi

The demon with the highest magic stat in the game. She also comes with a unique skill Lotus dance (60% chance to charm all enemies), given her high luck stat she's almost guaranteed to inflict charm on the enemy team.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Null Force) to cover her weakness.
  • Yellow (Defensive Cry) (Tarunda + Rakukaja), giving her more of a support utility.
  • Purple (Insanity), another valid choice in increasing her really high ailment chance (perhaps you could even charm a rock O-O).

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force. if her weakness isn't covered.
  • Megido/la/laon. To take advantage of her really high magic stat.
  • Madness/Insanity. If you really want guaranteed ailment infliction then you can transfer these too (though i think it may be a bit overkill anyhows haha). It would help against high luck demons like Zhong Kui and Alilat if its crucial that they are to be charmed.

Brands: Divine type to ensure turn one charms + if you do go with an aoe almighty skill, the ability to spam it. Support type for Yellow if you want her to be more defensive. Sick brands too are also good, 60% + 20% from sick + 20% insanity = guaranteed charm? though you'd have to factor in opponents luck and resistances anyhows.

Ixtab

The opposite of a tanky demon as instead of surviving as long as possible she'd prefer to die due to her unique passive Yaxche. This activates only on the enemy turn and has the effect of when she dies: her killer is hit by an almighty attack (100 power) and is killed with a 100% success rate. This bypasses Endure/Avatar/Enduring Soul, Tetraja and Seiran's Null Mortal, but doesn't activate if her team is wiped out/she's the last one to die. On offense you can easily exploit the game's AI to target her by intentionally leaving her at level 1, or having the least defense/hp/mdef so they're tempted to kill her straight away.

  • There are counters to her ability if you ever see her on defense teams:
    • Kill all other demons and leave her last.
    • Use Huang Long's special ability: 5 elements as this repels almighty attacks, blocking her ability.
    • Use ailments on her/her team. Poison can kill her without worry of her passive, charm can force her allies to kill her.
    • Bring Reiko as you'd probably expect them to use Recarm a lot. This will ensure Ixtab's ability only activates once as she should be easily killable

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow (Mahashibaboon + Insanity). Due to her really high luck stat, when she is alive she can function as a ailment caster to be an annoyance to the enemy team. Insanity complements this and levelling up her passive also increases her ailment infliction rate. Give her other ailments and she can pile them on the enemy team.
  • Purple (Berserker + Auto-Rakunda). Berserker, usually bad (+10% damage dealt, +20% damage taken) is good in her case as you'd want her to Die For Me. Auto-Rakunda also adds onto this making the enemy team worry more about their defenses at the start of the match. You can technically build her for magic if you want as berserker would make her hit harder. She has low strength so don't bother with phys skills/stats.

Recommended Skills:

  • Madness. When Yellow its a good complement to go with her.
  • Recarmdra, Samarecarm. Bring back allies if she's being targeted last
  • Any other ailment skills like Charm, Mute, Weak, etc.
  • Barrier break to remove barriers for ailment inflicting (though another demon can technically fulfil this role)

Brands: Divine/Ward, Speed/Ward, Spell/Ward, Lead/Lead, etc. Don't give her Life/def/mdef, instead focus on stats like Ailment Infliction, Ailment Resistance, Speed (If you want to act first), Matk % if you want to use her to attack.

Samael

Another 5* unit with access to Infinite Chakra. Naturally he also has pretty high stats (excluding strength). He comes with Salvation (same heal amount as Mediarama + prayer effect attached to it, 3 uses however), Mana Aid meaning he'd be a really effective pve demon and God's Malice a unique skill which can cause a myriad of ailments to a single target.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Infinite Chakra). He has costly skills and so this will help in usage and spamming. Combining this with divine brands will ensure you can two turn cast Salvation.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Elec to cover weakness
  • Megidolaon. Taking advantage of his high magic stat and his large mp regen too you can use this for sweeping and God's Malice to lock targets down.

Brands: Divine Type as mentioned before.

Seth

Second strongest demon in the game coming with Debilitate (Tarunda, Rakunda, Sukunda all in one) and his unique skill Ouas (really high damage and excellent crit rate, with the added 40% healing based on damage done). He's built to be a single target sniping unit.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Clear (Titanomachia). With the added crit chance and accuracy upon levelling, it can be a good sweeping skill for him if you don't want Hades Blast. Given it gets the -1mp cost treatment it'd cost the same.
  • Teal (Resist Fire). Covers his weakness you give you the freedom of customizing the rest of his skills.
  • Yellow (Nocturne). Though you'd want to be spamming Ouas, Nocturne can add more support to him. Debilitate + Nocturne would ensure you have the most defensive advantage shifted to you.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Fire if not covered by Teal.
  • Phys Boost to increase his damage further
  • Aoe skill, though not needed can be good for him too.

Brands: Divine Type so he can cast Debilitate and Nocturne easily and spam Ouas more often.

Barong

One of the worst 5* demons and mainly shifted to a more defensive role, he has two weaknessess which means you won't get much freedom in customizing him. He however comes with a unique skill Barong Dance which has the same power as Mediarama and casts Luster Candy when used (3 uses however). He has pretty low magic and it doesn't help that his other two skills are Mazionga and Bolt Storm meaning he'll be spamming lighting if your team is fully healed.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Null Dark), To cover one of his weaknesses. You also would need to cover force

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force to cover his second weakness
  • Ailment aoe skill to take advantage of his luck stat which is pretty high

Brands: Divine type so that he's able to cast Barong Dance when needed.

Siegfried

An amazing physical attacker with no weaknesses (Drains Phys). He utilizes Berserker God, Rebellion and a unique passive "Arms Mastery" (+15% phys damage/phys hit rate) making him effective at hitting his target without any problems or repercussions from reflected attacks. Given he naturally has Rebellion, he's also good when paired with Zaou Gongen, granting him phys pierce when he uses it.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Red and Yellow is decent but not that useful.
  • With Red (Titanomachia + Great Aim), Siegfried already has good accuracy so Great Aim wouldn't do that much and you can always transfer something like Hades Blast instead for damage.
  • Yellow (Charge + Auto-Sukunda), Charge is nice but you'd waste a lot of turns setting up both charge and rebellion. Auto-Sukunda is good but again his accuracy is already pretty good, that auto skill can be used from Yatagarasu anyhows.
  • If however you want to go with accuracy stacking then Red is best for the job, maxing out Titanomachia would help in hitting evasive demons such as Garuda and Yatagarasu. Since an aoe is given to you, you have two free slots to customize with whatever you want. If you run him in a Hell Biker setup, then you don't even need to worry about getting Savage glee for crits. Auto-sukunda from yellow would help for turn one wipes (meaning you don't have to bring a Yatagarasu yourself), whilst Great Aim would help in more accuracy in general.
  • Purple (Enduring Soul + Myriad Arrows). Adds to his survivability. Myriad arrows is also nice for bosses, though people prefer to just give him an Aoe instead.
  • Teal (Null Force + Resist Dark). The main reason i'm recommending this is that it makes him completely immune to Susanoo as it blocks all of his sources of attacks. It can also block Rangda's Mazandyne and since a lot of demons have force in their kits in the first place it can shut down a lot of sweeps. Resist Dark can be replaced.

Recommended skills:

  • An Aoe Skill since he doesn't come with one, Hades Blast is good.
  • Phys Boost for more damage
  • Savage/Bloody Glee for more criticals if you can't be bothered using rebellion
  • Endure if using Enduring soul to stack it up.

Brands: War/Ward or Divine/Ward, he works fine with both.

Ananta

His main use seen in pve due to his auto buffs. Auto Sukuka, Rakuka, (+Taruka when purple). Has pretty high health, vitality, agility meaning he's pretty good at tanking. Also is immune to ice, elec and light which is pretty cool o-o.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Auto-Taruka). Without it, he's still pretty good regardless as you can always get Auto-Taruka from another source. But this just ensures all 3 comes from him and you wouldn't need another demon just for auto-taruka. His other archetypes aren't that good anyways sadly.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force to cover weakness
  • Acid Breath/War Cry/Fog Breath. You can then ensure you got all buffs and debuffs up to swing the tables early onto your side.

Brands: Support Type or Divine Type. You wan't him to be as tanky as possible to survive as long as possible spamming debuffs.

Surt

The only source of Fire Pierce, but not that good of a demon as a fire weakness isn't that prevalent. He's mainly seen either with a purple archetype for Fire Pierce or Clear for Intimidating Stance. He comes with a unique skill Ragnarok which is another single target sniping skill (getting 20% damage based on Phys Attack).

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Fire Pierce). Enables you to choose any target you like to send Ragnarok at.
  • Clear (Intimidating Stance). For pvp mainly, removes a press turn if your opponent goes first. Would be paired with Zhong Kui.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Ice to cover weakness
  • Endure for pvp survival
  • Fire Boost if you want Ragnarok to be stronger
  • Maragi if using Fire Pierce and want an aoe skill.

Brands: Divine Type if you want to spam Ragnarok with Fire Pierce or Phys Type for Berserker God damage.

Thor

Quite tanky, has high strength and if purple is our Elec Pierce unit. He comes with a unique skill Mjolnir hitting 4-5 times with elec damage. Pair him up with Eileen to take advantage of her bonuses. He also has quite nice resistances, resisting phys and light, nulls fire and drains elec. Though on auto he'd attempt to use charge a lot so he's better for manual play.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Elec Pierce). So that he can rain down lighting to anyone and everyone who doesn't bring along Alilat/Rama.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force to cover his weakness.
  • Elec Boost to increase his mjolnir damage further

Brands: Divine type (focus on magic attack) so that he can spam Mjolnir.

Asherah

One of the tankiest healers in the game with high magic, hp and vitality stats. She however comes with two weaknesses and so you'd need to invest to cover it. She has Mediarama for party healing, maragion and a unique skill Mother's Creation which is essentially Samarecarm + Lydia in one.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Repel Ice) to cover her weakness and give you the freedom to customize a slot, whilst the other goes to covering dark weakness
  • Purple (Recovery Amp) to boost her healing even further. But then the other slots should be used for covering weaknesses meaning no extra skills to add.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Dark
  • Resist Ice if not teal.
  • Samarecarm to give her another resurrection chance.

Brands: Divine Type to ensure you can heal in time.

Mastema

Very useful in Sloth 10 farming as the main farming method is to wipe them out with light aoe. He comes with a unique skill Trial Of Hate which is an aoe light attack and applies a random Tarunda, Rakunda, Sukunda only when the attack isn't nulled/drained/repelled. He naturally goes well with Alilat as he repels force and light, get a Teal Mastema and you can also repel ice.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Repel Ice). Gives him another element that he can reflect, though the only ice pierce unit in the game is Purple Rama, so its quite a niche use.
  • Purple (Light Amp). Further strengthens his light damage allowing for more damage.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Fire to cover his weakness
  • Megido if you plan to use him outside of Sloth 10
  • Light Boost if you want to only use him in Sloth 10 or in fights where the enemy isn't immune to light.

Brands: Divine Type, more mana means more Trial of Hate Spamming.

Amaterasu

One of the best buffers in the game due to her unique skill Tenson Kourin being buffed, Tenson Kourin has the same effect as Luster Candy but casts Dekunda beforehand so you'd always have 3 buffs on you. She also has really high magic for healing and magic damage.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow (Mediarahan). With her high magic attack, she'd naturally have high heals too. With that she makes a really good support/healing unit.
  • Teal (Null Elec). You'd want to build her for damaging support instead as you'd get two free slots for magic skills.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Elec to cover weakness
  • Samarecarm for more support
  • Megido/la/laon for a damaging spell to take advantage of her magic
  • Any other magic elements

Brands: Divine Type to enable Tenson Kourin casts and Mediarahan if yellow.

Ishtar

Second strongest caster in the game with a unique skill Mesopotamian Star, hitting all enemies casting Tarukaja and Taruna. She's mainly used for a sweeping Jack Bros team with the Yellow Archetype but can also be used in Sloth 10 due to having Mahamaon. Concentrate, get the Jacks to tag then Mesopotamian to hopefully one shot the enemy or weaken them enough to finish them off the next turn/on the same turn. She comes with two weaknesses so there isn't that much customization available.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow (Concentrate). Use with Templar Dragon to ensure you get as much damage as possible.
  • Teal (Null Force). Gives you the freedom to customize another skill slot. If you got Concentrate from the skill gacha this would technically be a viable archetype.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Dark to cover her weakness
  • Resist Force if not teal
  • Whatever you like if Teal, her base kit is technically good enough. You can go Endure for survival or another element as well if you wished.

Brands: Divine Type to ensure Mesopotamian Star casts. I do believe Magic Type can work if you maxed our her skill for the reduced mp costs.

Susano-o

Single target sniper with really high strength due to Assassin (+10% bonus damage for single target skills), Mortal Jihad and Kusanagi. Kusanagi being his unique skill fully boosted by Phys ATK and also casting Tarukaja when not hitting null/repel/drain meaning you'd almost all of the time would have the damage buff active.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Clear (Strength Amp III) to further boost his damage. Boosting strength will increase Kusanagi damage and Mortal Jihad if the enemy is immune to force
  • Teal (Drain Elec) When paired with Rama he can stop Cu Chulainn and Thor in their tracks as their pierce will have no effect on him.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Fire to cover weakness
  • Force boost to increase Kusanagi damage further

Brands: Divine Type to ensure you can first turn Kusanagi/Mortal Jihad in pvp and spam it more in general. Though Phys Type will increase his damage way more.

Nergal

Phys oriented demon who can be paired with Chernobog to produce devastating results. He has amazing strength, agility and luck so he can dish out hits, crits all day. Naturally he has Berserker God and two unique skills: Meslamtea (+20% accuracy and casts rebellion whenever an enemy is poisoned), Pague of Babylon (Casts Barrier Break then Weak all with 80% infliction rate). With these two unique skills he can also be paired with Zaou Gongen to give his attacks a pierce effect. You'd use him to remove barriers first then inflict weak on enemies, once they're weakened they can easily be poisoned which results in him getting Rebellion. You'd want an aoe skill on him of course in order to sweep.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple can be decent with Auto Taraka and Strength Amp III, but you can always bring along other demons to Tarukaja your team anyways. There are a good amount of sources that come with it, and skills as well.
  • Red (Hades Blast + Savage Glee). A -1mp Hades Blast is really nice on him and paired with Savage Glee, without Rebellion he is really likely to crit. This also removes the need to transfer an aoe onto him, so you can cover his weakness and either leave it at that or replace Savage Glee with whatever you like.
  • Yellow (Toxic Sting + Auto-Sukuka). Toxic Sting naturally complements his passive. Assuming the enemy has no barriers, you can Toxic Sting them, poison them then have Rebellion casted on yourself and rinse and repeat. Auto-Sukuka helps in landing hits and increases evasion too so that's always a plus.

Recommended Skills:

  • Resist Ice, cover his weakness. Or not if you want to maximise his damage potential
  • Phys Boost, to increase his damage even further
  • Deadly Poison, goes well with his passive.

Brands: War/Ward works fine since he doesn't have any costly skills. Divine/Ward if you want a more costly aoe.

Black Frost

Dark sweeper with his unique skill Go To Hell!, hitting all enemies with 80% curse effect (This also dark pierces). He comes with a unique Jaki trait passive Curse Of Anguish which reduces damage taken from Cursed enemies and increases damage towards cursed enemies. He also has Mabufudyne for ice sweeping too (but his main focus should be dark in all honesty).

Best Archetype(s):

  • Yellow (Concentrate). Charge up and deal heaps of damage, self explanatory

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Light to cover weakness
  • Dark Boost to strengthen Go To Hell!.
  • Deadly curse could also work, but it's reliant on the enemy being cursed in the first place so you'd need to set it up with another demon.

Brands: Divine Type so he can spam his unique skill.

Cu Chulainn

Recommended sweeper and first 5* demon to fuse in the game. With Force Pierce (Purple only) and Mazandyne he can sweep any pve content like crazy (he's my main attacker in pvp too). He also has no weaknesses so you get to mix and match a variety of skills.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Force Pierce). No question, nothing really compares to pierce

Recommended skills:

  • Force Boost to increase his damage further
  • Force Amp if you got lucky and got it in the skill genome
  • Endure for pvp if you want survival
  • Mazan to finish off enemies/enables first turn aoe cast in pvp
  • Zanma L if you want a single target skill on him (Though this'll lock him into just casting it though, he'd never save up for Mazandyne)

Brands: Divine Type or Magic Type is valid. Divine Type allows him to two turn Mazandyne and spam magic more in general.

Quetzalcoatl

Demon that can be built to be tanky due to his resistances whilst using Teal (He resists all but ice and elec and is weak to dark, Teal gives him Repel Phys). His base kit consists of Maragidyne, Mazandyne and Ce Actis' Return (unique skill). It acts in the same manner as Recarmdra except the user doesn't die. However its coded to behave like Samarecarm so Quetz will try to cast it as soon as he sees at least one ally dead (so you can take advantage of the AI in that regard). Mainly used for pvp and when paired with Alilat (and being Teal) he can stop phys sweepers from taking down your team and bring them back to life when they fall too.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Repel Phys). Stops phys units.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Dark, cover that weakness
  • Samarecarm. Gives you another resurrection shot.
  • Endure. Gives him more survival.
  • Mind Break. He'd annoy people by draining their mp though i'm not sure whether he'd even try to save up for Ce Actis' then.

Brands: Support Type. He needs to be tanky and ideally the last demon to fall, so that he'd bring back everyone else.

Orcus

Demon designed and built for tankiness (Third highest hp/vitality in game) with two unique abilities centering around weaknesses: Law of Hell prevents the enemy team from getting extra press turn icons when a weakness is hit, whilst Hell's Gate grants damage reduction depending on the number of weak attributes your team has (3% per weakness, max 30%). Naturally he comes with War Cry for debuffing and 2 weaknesses himself, meaning by default you get a 6% damage reduction bonus (though this applies only to aoe/multi-target skills). Pairing him with Hecatoncheires would grant you an extra 15% damage reduction and if you don't cover Hecaton's weakness then in total they share 27% damage reduction. So if you do cap out on damage reduction (by bringing demons such as Slime, Abraxas, Arahabaki, etc) you'd get a total of 45% damage reduction, halving all damage done by the enemy. With this strat however, single target skills will still do standard damage to you.

There are counters to Orcus teams:

  • Use Loki to be able to have a chance at skipping press turn usage
  • Use Chalk Eater as your dx2, you'll still get buffs when you hit their weakness so you don't have to bring a buffer unit. There's also the chance of getting extra press turns

Best Archetype(s):

  • Red is quite bad to switch/shift archetype into (Mamudoon) as Orcus isn't really meant to be a dps (he has a meh magic stat anyways). Auto-Tarunda is a really good skill though, so if you pulled him as Red, i'd recommend switching to another archetype, keeping Auto-Tarunda.
  • Purple can have its uses (Tetra Shift + Hell Gaze), it can stop first turn phys, giving you one turn of breathing room, though in higher pvp people generally bring pierce phys demons anyhows. Hell Gaze is pretty bad using the same reasoning as Mamudoon.
  • Yellow (Nocturne + Null Bind). Gives him another supporting skill to use to enhance your team's defense and evasion. Coupled with the Tarunda effect from War Cry, once both buffs are up you'll be taking quite miniscule aoe damage. Null Bind is okay, but can be replaced if you desire to do so.
  • Teal (Null Phys + Vitality Amp III). Makes him immune to phys attacks which is really good in pve, in pvp it can have its uses as well for blocking damage from phys demons such as: Siegfried, Seth, Zhong Kui, Shiva (provided he doesn't Pralaya), Yoshitsune. When facing a Hell Biker team, you may be able to survive long enough for all 3 uses of Red Zone wear off, making him immune to their team, as they are oriented towards aoe phys.

Recommended Skills:

  • Endure/Enduring Soul can ensure he stays alive
  • Samarecarm/Recarmdra to bring back allies since your goal is to keep him alive as long as possible
  • Dekaja to be a counter against Ananta buffing via Tenson Kourin/Luster Candy

Brands: Life/Ward ensures he survives as long as possible, though Divine/Ward can work too so that he can spam out War Cry as much as possible and two turn Samarecarm/Recarmdra skills.

Garuda

Speediest demon in the game and really squishy. He has high luck and magic meaning he's built for evasion and turn one kill pvp teams. Repelling Fire and Nulling light means he'd be really useful in Vanity 10 where the final boss there is Quetzacoatl with Mazandyne and Maragidyne. Naturally he also comes with Auto-Rakuna and Vanaha (+15% evasion, +30% ailment resistance) further solidifying those points before. Given the ailment resistance boost he'd have low chances of being affected by ailments. Garuda in pvp can be used to help face against Hell Biker defense teams, with the highest agility in game + the bonus to evasion, he should be able to easily dodge phys attacks. Take Yellow for Fog Breath and somehow get sukukaja onto your team to ensure you won't get hit.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Purple (Berserker). +10% damage, +20% taken. He's going to die anyways so might as well boost his damage? You'd be aiming for sweeps.
  • Yellow (Fog Breath). For the main purpose of increasing evasion against the more phys inclined teams. This reduces the chances of you being hit by dangerous phys demons such as Huang Di, Mara, Siegfried, etc. Wouldn't be so good against magic teams however.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Ice, optional though. On the one hand covering his weakness would prevent opponents from getting extra turns, on the other he'll die anyways so at least they'd only get one extra turn.
  • Megido for sweeping Magic aoe for sweeping.

Brands: Divine Type since we want to be sweeping as quickly as possible. This will allow you to turn 1 Megido in pvp. Go for Magic Type if you wanna use him for Vanity 10 (you can then go with Mamudo). Building evasion is another way to go as aforementioned.

Lilith

Along with Barong she's another 5* which is not so good. She has two weaknesses meaning you'd need to cover them (and she'd be costly to invest into). She comes with a unique skill Allure that does dark damage to all and has a 35% chance to charm, this would be pretty good if only there weren't so many enemies immune to dark. Her pretty high luck means the charm would probably stick if enemies are hit.

Best Archetype(s):

  • Teal (Null Light). Gotta cover her weakness.

Recommended skills:

  • Resist Force, same reason as above.
  • Megido for more coverage
  • Dark boost for more allure damage

Brands: Magic type. Allure isn't that costly at all which is nice (6 mp cost), you can opt for Divine type if you like.
